Meet the team

Oscar Ericson

Oscar is our CTO. He comes from a large Swedish family and is therefore not originally from around here, which can sometimes make him a bit hard to understand – but there is absolutely nothing wrong with his mind, aside from a bit of ADD. He’s our technical genius and makes sure everything just works. With age on his side, Oscar has experienced quite a lot – he was involved in both the Korean War and the Vietnam War, and he was present when the computer was invented. It immediately sparked his interest, and since then, he has built up an enormous amount of knowledge. Oscar holds an education in programming, project management, embedded systems, and networking.

Email: oscar@hvalfangerne.com

Tobias Wagner Bissø

Tobias comes from central Zealand, where he grew up in peace and quiet – though with dyslexia. He is our HR Director, fully focused on the well-being and development of our employees. Since we are a small company, he also reports to our CTO, Oscar. Tobias holds a foundational education in Python, networking, business management, and embedded systems. Combined with a humanities-based background in understanding people, he plays a vital role in the company.

Email: wagner@hvalfangerne.com

Since we’re a small company, he also works closely with our CTO, Oscar. With a background in programming, networking, project management, and embedded systems, Mohammed is a vital part of the company.
