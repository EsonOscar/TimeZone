A user friendly time tracking system, keeping employee work times and equipment usage organised.

Android App

Employees log into the companion app, and go to work as normal. The app tracks time worked automatically.

Equipment Usage Statistics

When employees use equipment, the system registers how long it's used, and by whom.

Automated Workflow

The system does all of the heavy lifting for you, tracking, storing and sorting time worked and how, securely.

Administrator Dashboard

As an administrator, you have easy access to all of your employees work times and machine usage.

How does it work our app?

Well to put it blunt, it's to make the user able to keep track of time. A better overview to see how much the individuel has worked as a form of a check in and check out system. Furthermore making it easier for people who has a hard time of reading or writing to simply tap a button and the app does the work for them.

Equipment Usage Statistics what does it mean?

With the help of our companion app, the system registers the users daily work hours. It also registers how long certain machinery has been used with the help of our BLE beacons that connects to the phone and through that sends the data to the administrators dashboard.

What does Automated workflow mean?

As mention before out system does the heavy lifting, which includes storing, sorting and tracking all the time that the employee and machinery has been spent. Which afterwards is being kept safe and secure for only the administrator to see.

The Administrators Dashboard

As the high and mighty Administrator, you get your own Dashboard which includes an easy access to all of you're employees work hours and machinery usages. You'll get to have a full overview of hours spent for everything and everyone.
